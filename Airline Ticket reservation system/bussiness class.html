Business Class Details







Business class is a premium cabin class offered by airlines, providing passengers with a luxurious and comfortable travel experience. With spacious seating and extra legroom, it offers a higher level of comfort compared to economy class. Business class passengers enjoy priority boarding and check-in, allowing them to skip the long queues and save time at the airport. The in-flight services and amenities are enhanced, with gourmet meals, premium beverages, and personalized attention from the cabin crew. Access to airport lounges provides a tranquil space to relax before the flight, offering complimentary food and drinks, Wi-Fi, and other amenities. Business class also offers additional baggage allowance and priority baggage handling, ensuring a seamless travel experience. Whether traveling for business or leisure, business class provides passengers with a premium and enjoyable journey.
