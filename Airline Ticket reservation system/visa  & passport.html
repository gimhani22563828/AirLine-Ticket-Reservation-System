Visa And Passport Requirment






  1. Obtaining visa for the country of transit and country of destination remains the responsibility of the passenger.
  2. A valid passport and a valid visa for the country of destination is required for the duration of the trip and the expiry date of these documents must be in accordance with the countries the passenger will be travelling to. Please note that certain countries require a 6 months passport validity period in order for you to travel.
  3. A transit visa may be required as per the immigration laws of that transit country of destination (for example if a passenger is switching airline carriers at a transit destination or if the period of transit exceeds a certain time period or where the main checked baggage has not been interlined to the final destination and needs to be re-checked in).
  4. For more information on passport and visa information, please contact the immigration authorities of the country of transit and country of destination.
